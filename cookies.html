Our cookie policy

We use cookies to make your experience better — and we're completely open about how we use them. This page explains what cookies we set, why, and how you can control them.

🔐 Your privacy matters. We use essential & analytics cookies to improve our site. You can choose which cookies you accept.

Cookies we use

🍪 Essential always active

Necessary for the site to function. They enable basic features like page navigation and secure access.

Duration: session · Type: first-party

📊 Analytics optional

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site — anonymously. We use this to improve content and design.

Duration: 1 year · Type: first-party

🎨 Preferences optional

Remember your choices, like language or region, so we can personalise your experience.

Duration: 6 months · Type: first-party

📢 Marketing optional

Used to deliver relevant content and promotions. We never sell your data — these are set by trusted partners.

Duration: 30 days · Type: third-party

How we handle cookies

  • Strictly necessary — these are always on, no consent needed.
  • Performance & analytics — we use them to count visits and see which pages are popular.
  • Functionality — remember your settings so you don’t have to set them again.
  • Targeting & advertising — we limit these; you can opt out at any time.

You can change your cookie preferences anytime by clicking the “Manage preferences” link in the footer or the cookie bar above. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
