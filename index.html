    Frequently asked

    What is the best mulch for vegetables?

    Straw, leaf mold, or compost in layers. Avoid fresh wood chips near annuals.

    How do I start a no-dig garden?

    Layer cardboard, compost, and mulch. Plant directly into the compost layer.

    When should I fertilize?

    Base dressing at planting, then side-dress at peak growth for heavy feeders.
